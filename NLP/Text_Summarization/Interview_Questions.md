# Text Summarization - Interview Questions

## Q1: What are the two types of text summarization?

1. Extractive: Select important sentences from original text
2. Abstractive: Generate new sentences that capture meaning

## Q2: What is the difference?

- Extractive: Copy-paste from original text
- Abstractive: Paraphrase and generate new text

## Q3: What models are used for summarization?

1. Sequence-to-sequence models
2. Transformers (BART, T5, PEGASUS)
3. Pre-trained language models fine-tuned for summarization

## Q4: What is ROUGE?

ROUGE (Recall-Oriented Understudy for Gisting Evaluation) measures summarization quality by comparing n-grams with reference summaries.

## Q5: Applications?

- News summarization
- Document review
- Meeting notes
- Legal document summary
