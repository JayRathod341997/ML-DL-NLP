# BERT Interview Questions

## Q1: What is masked language modeling?

**Answer:** During pre-training, random tokens are masked and the model learns to predict them from context.

## Q2: Why is BERT bidirectional?

**Answer:** Uses Transformer encoder which attends to all positions simultaneously, allowing context from both directions.

## Q3: What is the [CLS] token?

**Answer:** Classification token whose output is used for classification tasks.
