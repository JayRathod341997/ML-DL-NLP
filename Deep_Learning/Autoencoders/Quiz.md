# Autoencoders Quiz

## True/False

### Q1: Autoencoders require labeled data.
**Answer:** False - they're unsupervised.

### Q2: Latent space is the compressed representation.
**Answer:** True.

### Q3: Decoder expands latent space to output.
**Answer:** True.

### Q4: Undercomplete autoencoders always overfit.
**Answer:** False - they learn useful representations.

### Q5: Denoising autoencoders remove noise from input.
**Answer:** True.

---

## Multiple Choice

### Q6: What does the encoder do?
A) Reconstructs data  B) Compresses data  C) Adds noise  D) Evaluates
**Answer:** B

### Q7: Loss function is typically:
A) Accuracy  B) MSE  C) Cross-entropy only  D) F1
**Answer:** B

### Q8: Overcomplete autoencoder has:
A) Small latent space  B) Large latent space  C) No latent space  D) Fixed
**Answer:** B

### Q9: Used for anomaly detection?
A) Yes  B) No  C) Only with labels  D) Never
**Answer:** A

### Q10: Variational autoencoder learns:
A) Only reconstruction  B) Probability distribution  C) Classification  D) Clustering
**Answer:** B
