# Topic Modeling

## Explain Like I'm 5

Imagine you have a huge pile of newspapers! Instead of reading all of them, you want to know what topics they cover - like Sports, Politics, Entertainment!

Topic modeling is like finding hidden themes in a collection of documents automatically!

## What is Topic Modeling?

Finding abstract "topics" in a collection of documents.

### Common Algorithms:

1. **LDA (Latent Dirichlet Allocation)**
   - Most popular
   - Each document is mixture of topics
   - Each topic is mixture of words

2. **NMF (Non-negative Matrix Factorization)**
   - Faster than LDA
   - Produces interpretable topics

## Where Used?

1. **Document Organization**: Organize large document collections
2. **Content Recommendation**: Suggest related content
3. **Market Research**: Analyze customer feedback
4. **News Categorization**: Group news articles

## Example:

Document: "Sports team wins championship"
Document: "Stock market analysis"
Document: "Sports scores update"

Topic 1: Sports (team, championship, scores)
Topic 2: Finance (stock, market, analysis)

## Enterprise Example:

**Google News** groups articles by topic automatically for easier browsing!
